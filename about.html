About Us

Our native speakers are dedicated to improving pronounciation, comprehension, and building confidence in foreign speakers striving to attain fluency in English. Classes for Change is a non-profit organization that offers a chance for real-life conversation practice, cultural exchange and understanding, and immediate feedback on language usage, grammar, and pronunciation. Email us and schedule free calls with our native speakers today and gain command of the English language!
